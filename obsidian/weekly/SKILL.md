Review ideas captured over the last 7 days and synthesize patterns.

## Steps

1. **Find recent ideas** — search the vault for all notes with `type: idea` modified or created in the last 7 days. Collect their filenames, tags, status, and related links.

2. **Read each idea** — read the content of every found note.

3. **Find clusters** — group ideas by shared themes, not just tags. Look for ideas that link to each other or share a common underlying question. Name each cluster with a short phrase.

4. **Find loners** — ideas with no related links or that don't fit any cluster. These are either seeds of something new or noise.

5. **Suggest promotions** — from the `raw` ideas, pick 1–2 that seem most developed or emotionally charged. These are candidates to upgrade to `status: growing`. Don't change the status automatically — just name them and explain why.

6. **Pose one question** — based on everything you've seen, formulate one sharp question that would be worth sitting with next week. Not a task, not an action item — a question.

7. **Write the report** — create a note `Итоги недели YYYY-MM-DD.md` (use today's date) with this structure:

```markdown
---
type: weekly
tags: [weekly]
date: YYYY-MM-DD
---

## Кластеры

### [Название кластера]
[1–2 sentences on what connects these ideas]
- [[идея 1]]
- [[идея 2]]

## Одиночки
- [[идея]] — [one sentence on what it might connect to]

## Кандидаты в growing
- [[идея]] — [why]

## Вопрос недели
[The question]
```

8. **Report back** — show the report content in the response so the user sees it immediately without opening the file.
